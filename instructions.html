How to Participate

Welcome to the Digits N Data Challenge! This is a 6-week journey where you solve SQL questions, unlock the next one, and showcase your creativity. Here’s how it works:

Step 1: Stay Updated

Follow Digits N Data, Nitish, and Muskan on LinkedIn. This ensures you don’t miss any updates or hints about the challenge.

Step 2: Solve the Questions

There will be 6 questions, one per week. Each question is like a puzzle—you solve it using SQL queries.

Step 3: Share Your Answers

Post your query on LinkedIn and tag us. Only after submitting your answer will the next question unlock for you.

Step 4: Be Creative with Your Final Submission

After completing all 6 questions, you have 2 days to present your work creatively. You can make a video, presentation, or any creative format to show how you solved the queries. Post this on LinkedIn or YouTube and tag us. We’ll check it out and analyze your work.

Step 5: Show Your Creativity

This challenge isn’t just about getting the answers right. We want to see how you express your work, your style, and your creativity.
